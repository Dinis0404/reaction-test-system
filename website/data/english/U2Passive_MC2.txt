题目1：A language ______ learnt only by reading about it. You have to practice it.  
A. can't be  
B. couldn't have been  
答案：A  
解析：句子意为「一种语言____只通过阅读学会。你必须练习它」，一般现在时被动，只有 “can't be”（不能被）符合，表示不可能。

---

题目2：These jeans ______ before you wear them. The material will be softer and more comfortable.  
A. should be washed  
B. should have been washed  
答案：A  
解析：句子意为「这些牛仔裤____在你穿之前。材质会更软更舒服」，情态动词被动建议，只有 “should be washed”（应该被洗）符合。

---

题目3：Taxes ______ on or before April 15th. Payments ______ to the government on or before April 15th.  
A. have to pay / must be sent  
B. have to be paid / must have been sent  
答案：B  
解析：句子意为「税____在4月15日前。付款____政府在4月15日前」，被动语态义务，只有 “have to be paid / must have been sent”（必须被支付 / 必须已被发送）符合时态与逻辑。

---

题目4：The senator has made a good point, but I disagree. May I ______ to speak now?  
A. permit  
B. be permitted  
答案：B  
解析：句子意为「参议员说得好，但我不同意。我可以____现在发言吗？」，被动语态请求许可，只有 “be permitted”（被允许）符合礼貌表达。

---

题目5：Our kitchen is old and dark. We're going to renovate it. It ______ a light shade of green or white to make it look brighter.  
A. ought to be painted  
B. ought to paint  
答案：A  
解析：句子意为「我们的厨房旧而暗。我们要翻新。它____浅绿或白色让它看起来更亮」，被动语态建议，只有 “ought to be painted”（应该被漆成）符合。
